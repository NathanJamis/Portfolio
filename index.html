About Me

Geek. Gamer. Courier. Coder. Husband. Father. Adventurer.

There are many titles that I would use to describe myself, but the most important one is me!

Hello there, I am Nathan! During the day, I drive around delivering packages at FedEx. But when I get home, I spend time with my favorite people in the galaxy: my family! I have an amazing wife, a wonderful daughter, and a son who will grace the Earth with his presence in the coming few months. My family is the reason I started on the path to becoming a web developer. They knew that I needed to start finding a career path that would fit me better (and be easier on my body) than my current line of work. I have always had an interest and fascination in coding and computers, and I still have yet to truly decide where I would like to take my path, because the options seem endless!

However, when I'm not balancing all of the above, I find comfort in my geeky hobbies and interests. I love gaming of all types, whether it be board, card, or video games. My biggest obsession of all is Star Wars, and I have no shame admitting it. May the Force be with you!
